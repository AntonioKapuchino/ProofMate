About ProofMate

ProofMate is an innovative web service for automatic verification of solutions to linear algebra problems, designed specifically for educators and students working with Jupyter notebooks.

Our advanced verification algorithms analyze step-by-step solutions in .ipynb format, providing detailed feedback, error detection, and suggested corrections for matrices, vector spaces, linear transformations, and more.
